Ford California Market Analysis

This project aims to analyze the California market for Ford using official car sales numbers, Ford dealership locations, and counties economic and social metrics. The aim is to provide insights on where Ford should open new dealerships. This simulates a scenario for a Ford sales analyst or a competitor's analyst anticipating Ford's next move.
